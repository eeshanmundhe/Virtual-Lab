About Our Institution


The K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering (KJSCE) was established in 1983 at Somaiya Vidyavihar, and affiliated with the University of Mumbai. It offers 4 year bachelor's degree courses in the departments of Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology and Mechanical Engineering. KJSCE is an Autonomous college affiliated to the University of Mumbai spread across approx. 65 acres of posh land and stands among the top 3 engineering colleges in Mumbai. It is also one of the only 5 autonomous engineering colleges in Mumbai the others being VJTI, SP, DJ and Thakur.

Infrastructure

The College is housed In Two Buildings. The main building, known as Aryabhat, has Ground Four floors while the new building, known as Bhaskaracharya has Ground +Six floors. The college has an ultra-modern Air Conditioned Auditorium with 950 seating capacity which provides an advantage forthe conduction of important academic presentations and studentfunctons. Additionally, every engineering department has two seminar halls one each for B.Tech & M.Tech studems. All Seminar Hats and all class rooms are equipped with modem projection facillbes. Central and Departmental Libraries, wet equipped state-of-art Laboratories along with ample Computing facilities are the pride of this College. Amongst student amenities, an Indoor Badminton Hall, Girls common moms and a wall maintained spacious canteen facility caters to needs of all students on college Campus. Both the building are friendly to differently abled students.
